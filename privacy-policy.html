Privacy Policy

Last updated: March 17, 2026

This Privacy Policy describes how Mahal AI Companion ("we", "us", or "our") collects, uses, and shares information about you when you use our mobile application.

Information We Collect

We collect the following information:

  • Account Information: Email address when you create an account.
  • User Content: Messages and conversations you have within the app, memories you save, and photos you upload.
  • Device Information: Camera access when you choose to upload photos.
  • Purchase Information: Subscription and payment information processed through Apple's App Store.

How We Use Your Information

  • To provide and improve the app experience
  • To process subscription payments
  • To store your preferences and conversation history
  • To respond to your support requests

Data Storage

Your conversation data and memories are stored locally on your device using Apple's SwiftData framework. Some data may be processed through our secure backend servers to generate AI responses.

Third-Party Services

We use the following third-party services:

  • Groq AI: To process and generate AI responses. Your messages are sent to Groq's servers for processing.
  • Apple In-App Purchases: To handle subscription payments.

Data Sharing

We do not sell your personal information. We do not share your data with third parties except as necessary to provide the service (e.g., AI processing) or as required by law.

Your Rights (CCPA/CPRA)

As a California resident, you have the right to:

  • Know what personal information we collect
  • Delete your personal information
  • Opt-out of the sale of personal information (we do not sell data)
  • Non-discrimination for exercising your rights

CalOPPA Compliance

We agree to the following: Users can visit our app anonymously. Our Privacy Policy link is clearly visible. Users will be notified of any privacy policy changes on this page. Users are able to change their personal information by contacting us.

Children's Privacy

This app is intended for users 18 years of age and older. We do not knowingly collect information from children under 13.

Data Retention

We retain your data for as long as your account is active or as needed to provide services. You may request deletion of your data at any time by contacting us.
